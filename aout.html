Xela Cartel is a streetwear brand that originated in Lagos, Nigeria. Founded by a group of young entrepreneurs with a passion for fashion, the brand has quickly gained a loyal following for its high-quality products and unique designs.

At Xela Cartel, we believe that streetwear should be more than just a fashion statement - it should be a lifestyle. That's why we're dedicated to creating clothing that not only looks good but feels good to wear. Our products are made from the finest materials and are designed to withstand the rigors of everyday life.

Our journey started in Lagos, but we've since expanded to other cities and countries around the world. Despite our growth, we remain committed to our roots and continue to draw inspiration from the vibrant street culture of Nigeria.

We're proud to have you as family and a costumer.
